01  —  The Problem

When a cable fails,
everything stops.

Current HTS cables have no way to isolate a faulty section. The entire line must shut down for any intervention.

—
Entire line offline immediately No isolation capability exists today
—
Liquid nitrogen is hazardous Specialist crew & long site preparation required
—
No testability for R&D Cannot isolate segments to research improvements or degradation
02  —  Our Solution

SPLICE

An isolation connector between cable segments.

Close two SPLICE connectors to isolate a single section. Drain it, swap it, restore — while the rest of the line stays live and cold throughout.
